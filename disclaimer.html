Not a calibration tool, not a substitute for your firmware

Every chart re-implements a piece of Klipper/Kalico firmware math in client-side JavaScript so it can be explored interactively. These are best-effort reproductions for illustration purposes — they can contain bugs, drift out of sync with upstream firmware changes, or simply behave slightly differently from the actual C/Python code running on your printer's MCU and host. Never treat a number, recommendation, or generated config snippet from this site as a replacement for running the real SHAPER_CALIBRATE, TEST_RESONANCES, or any other in-firmware command, and always sanity-check any config change on your printer before trusting it unattended.
