Shaper tuning

Load a resonance capture and everything on this page is computed from your data: the measured spectrum, the shaper SHAPER_CALIBRATE would recommend (the full find_best_shaper search ported to your browser), a hands-on explorer for any shaper type, and a ready-to-paste [input_shaper] config.
Nothing is uploaded anywhere — peak detection, damping estimation, and the whole shaper search run locally.

Setting up a new printer? See the config reference for every changed [input_shaper] and [resonance_tester] option.

Want to try this on your own printer? Download the multimode-shaping-v3 branch for testing.
On the Klipper and Kalico BE tiers each shaper (or smoother) targets a single resonance, so it can't cancel several peaks at once.
This tool keeps your tallest peak and sets the rest aside — click any peak marker in the measured-spectrum legend below to include or exclude it.
To shape multiple resonances at once, use the Multimode tier.
