The recommendation ports Kalico's find_best_shaper (shaper_calibrate.py) — every smoother/shaper candidate plus a 2-peak multimode search with the dual-peak-cluster-widening fix, scored with the real formula. Multimode shaper = convolution of a base shaper tuned to each configured peak (amplitudes multiply, times add). Peak detection and half-power damping estimation port _detect_resonance_peaks / _estimate_damping_ratio. The fitted extruder kernel reproduces extruder_smoother.get_multi_mode_extruder_smoother (Legendre basis, KKT-constrained least squares). Rendered with Chart.js.
