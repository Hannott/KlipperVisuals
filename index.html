KlipperVisuals

Interactive visualizations of motion-control internals in Klipper / Kalico — pressure advance, input shaping, and resonance compensation.

Nonlinear pressure advance explorertanh vs recipr

Compare the two nonlinear pressure-advance models. Adjust ADVANCE, OFFSET, VELOCITY and a test move to see advance-vs-velocity, extruder flow rate, and acceleration demand, with the firmware's parabolic extruder smoother and a time-offset control.

Input shaper response explorerbranch vs upstream

Residual-vibration curves for every input shaper (ZV, MZV, ZVD, EI, 2hump/3hump EI), reproduced from shaper_defs.py. Overlays your branch against upstream for the 3hump_ei coefficient bug fix, with an impulse view and a damping-ratio sweep showing where upstream's truncated polynomial degrades.
Note on provenance. The pressure-advance page models a custom bleeding-edge-v2 implementation (tanh/recipr nonlinear PA) that is not in upstream Kalico or Klipper. The input-shaper page contrasts your branch against upstream/bleeding-edge-v2. More resonance-compensation comparisons (smoothers, resonance tester) are being added.

Math and kernels reproduced from klippy/chelper/kin_extruder.c, klippy/extras/extruder_smoother.py, and klippy/extras/shaper_defs.py. Charts rendered with Chart.js (CDN). Source: Hannott/KlipperVisuals.
